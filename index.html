How We Began

Estel Children’s Centre was founded in 2007 with a humble beginning of just one child. With time, the family grew from one to two, and today, we are proud to care for 66 children. The centre was established by Esther Wambui Waweru, a devoted Christian whose compassion and strong faith inspired her to reach out and support the less fortunate in society. Located in Kayole, Nairobi, Kenya, Estel Children’s Centre continues to be a place of love, care, and hope, where every child is nurtured and given a chance for a brighter future.

Our Mission

At Estel Children’s Centre, our mission is to provide a safe, loving, and supportive home for vulnerable children. We are committed to nurturing their physical, emotional, and spiritual growth by offering quality care, education, and guidance. Through compassion and faith, we strive to empower every child to reach their full potential and become responsible, confident, and hope-filled members of society.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing and empowering environment where every child is given the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive, becoming a confident, responsible, and self-reliant individual who contributes positively to society.
